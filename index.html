Elaina Price

Redefining Expertise in Real Estate

Elaina is the Principal and Co-Founder of Ethos Properties, a real estate partnership formed in 2014, formerly as E & J Ventures. Leveraging her background in corporate real estate, she grew the business from a passion project into a robust investment and development portfolio.

Her 20+ year career in complex commercial real estate—notably within the demanding sectors of oil and gas operations—shapes her deep understanding of surface use, mineral ownership, severed estates, and intricate title mechanics. This rigorous analytical foundation gives Elaina a rare command over property assets that far exceeds standard industry boundaries.

Running parallel to her technical real estate expertise is a decades-long creative endeavor in photography, interior rehabilitation, and landscape design. This unique synergy allows her to analyze an asset from the ground up, navigating complex legal frameworks while executing the comprehensive interior and exterior curation needed to realize a property's full potential.

Whether executing new visions for her own growing portfolio or advising best-in-class industry operators, her exacting standards and unmatched skill remain at the helm of what makes her stand out.
